If you’re looking to study or immigrate to an English-speaking country, then you’ll need to take the IELTS. The IELTS, or the International English Language Testing System, is a test that measures your ability to use and understand the English language in an academic setting. It covers the four main language skills: reading, listening, speaking, and writing.
