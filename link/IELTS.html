The IELTS, or the International English Language Testing System, is a test that measures your ability to use and understand the English language in an academic setting. It covers the four main language skills: reading, listening, speaking, and writing. De-Brill Concept is the perfect tool for those who need to improve their IELTS score for study or immigration purposes.
