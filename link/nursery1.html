    Blended Phonics

    ₦1,500.00 ₦1,300.00

    The book that sets your child on a journey to early reading. It is available both in soft copy and hard copy.

    Category: Pre-Nursery

    Description

    The Blended Phonics book is designed to help children gain confidence in reading through learning of the letter sounds. The book offers a variety of exercises that will help children access all the lessons on each letter sound. There are child-centred activities especially for this age and category of learners. Each letter sound is accompanied with relatable rhymes and enthralling stories for little children. All rhymes in this book are drawn from Jolly Phonics. Pictures and actions for the letter sounds have been carefully arranged to aid quick learning of the lessons. Children can also attempt writing the letter sounds by tracing the dotted lines in each letter sound. There are also activities that teach them about recognition and identification through sorting method. Children sort out the letter sounds with the use of pictures.

    In addition, learners in this category practise tracing letter sounds. This practice helps their grips on pencils to be firm. This is a book that teaches your child all he/she needs to gain firm foundation in reading and writing.
